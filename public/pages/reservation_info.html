Reservation Info

You can reserve tickets for any event you like from event detail page, just press the "reserve tickets" button. In case you need to update your reservation, repeat the process, selecting a different amount of tickets. For deleting any reservation, access your reservations page by clicking your name on the navbar and press the cancel button on the one you want to delete.

NOTE: you need to be logged in to reserve tickets or check reservations.
